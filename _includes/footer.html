About

Software Engineer at a Long Island based software development company, Francesco has been primarily focused on ASP.NET development, building custom enterprise e-commerce solutions and implementing and supporting custom Sitecore CMS solutions.

More recently, he has been exploring the world of Single Page Applications, built using AngularJS, and the fascinating possibilities offered by the amazing Neo4J graph database.

When he is not in front of his computer, you can find Francesco either behind one of his old cameras taking pictures, reading his growing photo book collection, trying for the 10th time to learn Japanese, cooking a spicy curry or walking his English cocker spaniels around town. He loves taking long road trips with his wife and his dream is to, one day, retire in Japan, living in a traditional Japanese house with a view of the Fuji mountain.
